Our Culture

We move fast, stay curious, and care deeply.

At PalmPay, you won't just execute — you'll have a seat at the table. Every team member is encouraged to challenge assumptions, propose solutions, and own their outcomes.

We're a flat-ish organisation where ideas win on merit, not tenure. We disagree openly, decide clearly, and move quickly.

⚡
Bias for Action
We value progress over perfection and ship things that make a difference.
🎯
Radical Ownership
No finger-pointing. If it's broken, you help fix it, regardless of whose problem it technically is.
🌱
Customer Obsession
Our users are the reason we exist. Every decision starts by asking: does this make their lives better?

Hiring Process

What to expect when you apply

Our process is designed to be fair, transparent and respectful of your time.

1
Apply Online
Submit your CV and a short note on why you're excited about the role.
2
Recruiter Screen
A 30-minute call with our talent team to discuss your background and expectations.
3
Technical / Skills Round
A practical assessment tailored to your role — usually a take-home or live session.
4
Final Interviews & Offer
Meet the team, ask all your questions, and receive a decision within 5 business days.
